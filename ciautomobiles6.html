Check out the 5 best Nigerian made cars with affordable prices.

The automobile industry plays an important part in the economic activities of all countries. Moreover, it’s also appreciated as the essential sector of the global economy. Generally, industrial countries are able to apply their accessible resources and advanced technology to manufacture vehicles. That enables these countries to create much more jobs, enhance the quality of life and drive the country’s economy forward.

Nigeria can't afford being left behind in the global pursuit of industrial enterprise and economic relevance. The country has set up numerous native automotive assembly and manufacture plants. Moreover, the government has adopted favourable laws along with tax policies to encourage the national auto industry to develop.

In 2013, the Nigerian government launched a new automobile policy which discouraged the import of entirely assembled vehicles. As the result, car manufacturers have seized the chance to launch more locally designed and assembled vehicles. Naijauto would like to bring you top five made-in-Nigeria cars you can take into consideration as follows:

The Kia Optima cars come with a sleek and ultra-modern design, aggressive front fascia and dynamic sculpted backside. 3. Hyundai Grand i10

The Stallion Group has announced that their made-in-Nigeria Hyundai car had officially gone on sale in the country with the price of from N1.5 million to N1.9 million.

Sunil Vaswani, Stallion Group Chairman said the car, which was assembled at the plant of Hyundai Motor in Lagos had affordable prices. That would stop the importation of automobile in Nigeria.

The Hyundai passenger vehicle with the price ranging from N1.5 million and N1.9 million are:

  • Hyundai Grand
  • Hyundai I10
  • Hyundai Accent
  • Hyundai IX35
  • Hyundai Elantra

The truck and bus cars are also supplied to the Nigeria market now. They include:

  • Three-ton HD65
  • Seven-ton HD 120
  • Ten-ton HD160
  • Five-ton HD78
  • 30-seater Stallion County bus
  • 28+1 seater Hyundai County bus

The Hyundai passenger vehicle has the price ranging from NGN1.5 million and NGN1.9

Tokunbo Aromolaran, Managing Director of Hyundai Motors Nigeria stated that the Hyundai cars met ISO/TS 16949, the highest global automotive operating standard. Moreover, Stallion Motors dealers located in Lagos, Asaba, Warri, Abakaliki and Anambra would provide various options of cars whose price below N2 million to Nigerians.
